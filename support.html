Support

Welcome to the support page for ButterflySprint.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I remove ads? Tap the settings menu in the game and select "Remove Ads." This will make a one-time purchase through the App Store.
  • Why am I seeing ads? Ads are shown to support development. If you purchased the "Remove Ads" option but still see ads, try restoring purchases from the settings menu.
  • Which devices are supported? ButterflySprint works on iPhone and iPad running iOS 13.0 or later.
